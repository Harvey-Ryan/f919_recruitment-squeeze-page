Meet the Crew

SCROLL

Fleet 919 is a North America based Star Citizen org with members spread across timezones. We make room for all play styles — whether that's Industrial, FPS, Pilots, or any other game loop Star Citizen has to offer.

No time-in-game requirements. Discord is required and voice chat is preferred. We're open to new players and veterans alike who are looking for a down to earth gaming community that understands real life takes precedence.

FAQ

Do I have to fly?

No, we're happy to chauffeur you around.

Are there ranks to climb?

No. Fleet 919 is built around the idea that no single player is more important than another. This is a community of friends simply playing a game together.

Do you run events?

Yes. Fleet 919 typically schedules larger events in the evenings on the weekends when the state of the game allows it.
